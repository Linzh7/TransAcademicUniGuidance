# Asia

In Asia, countries that criminalize transgender expression of gender identity include Brunei, Indonesia, Lebanon, Malaysia, Myanmar, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, and the United Arab Emirates [^1].

[^1]: ["Transgender Travel Guide: Could You Be Breaking the Law While on Vacation?" - Hong Kong Transgender Legal Database](https://www.hktranslawdb.org/post/travelrisks)
